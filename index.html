Welcome,

I am a postdoctoral fellow working in the D’Esposito Cognitive Neuroscience Lab at the Helen Wills Neuroscience Institute, UC Berkeley. My research focuses on brain network dynamics and cognitive control. I also have a strong interest in researching how brain connectivity develops from childhood to adolescence to support cognitive development. I am funded by an NIH NRSA F32 postdoctoral fellowship.

Beofre coming to Berkeley, I recevied my PhD in Cognitive Psychology and Cognitive Neuroscience from the University of Pittsburgh, working with Dr. Beatriz Luna. During my PhD study, I was a member of the Center for the Neural Basis of Cognition, a joint research training program between Pitt and CMU.

Before that, I was a Master's student with Dr. Ralph-Axel Mueller at San Diego State University.

I was born and raised in Taiwan.

You can learn more about my work by visiting the "research" or "publication" page.
