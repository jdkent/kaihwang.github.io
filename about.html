Welcome!

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at UC Berkeley with Mark D'Esposito. I did my PhD in Cognitive Psychology and Cognitive Neuroscience at the University of Pittsburgh with Beatriz Luna. Before that, I earned my M.A. from San Diego State and my B.S. in Psychology from National Chung-Cheng University, Taiwan,

My research focuses on brain network mechanisms of cognitive control and its developmental process. You can learn more about my work by reading my research page or publications.

In the Fall of 2018 I will be starting my own laboratory at The University of Iowa. I will be recruiting talented post-docs, graduate students, and research assistants. Please contact me if you are interested.
